What is Shamanic Counseling?

Shamanic Counseling is a process of self-discovery and inner healing that combines the principles of modern counseling with the ancestral wisdom of shamanism. Rather than seeking answers from the counsellor, you are guided to find them within yourself by entering an expanded state of consciousness — similar to a deep meditation — through the rhythmic sound of the shamanic drum.

During this state of relaxation and inner focus, you may receive insights, images, sensations, or symbolic messages that help you better understand your emotions, challenges, and life path. The shamanic counsellor acts as a facilitator: preparing the space, guiding the process safely, and helping you interpret and integrate what has been experienced.

This is a spiritual, not religious, practice and can be experienced by anyone who wishes to reconnect with their inner wisdom, heal emotional aspects, and strengthen their own energy and purpose.

How the Shamanic Counseling Session Works

The session lasts approximately 2 hours, divided into four main stages of about 30 minutes each:

1. Opening and Intention (30 minutes)

  • A space for you to share your intention with the counsellor.
  • The intention may be refined together to make it clearer and more focused, if needed.

2. Shamanic Journey (30 minutes)

  • You will be invited to lie down on a mat on the floor to help with grounding. If you have physical limitations, a sofa can be used instead.
  • To the sound of the shamanic drum, the journey begins. You will remain in a deep state of relaxation, describing aloud any images, sensations, or messages that arise.
  • The counsellor does not interact verbally during the journey unless you fall asleep — in that case, you may be gently touched on the shoulder to return.
  • This part will be recorded in audio.

3. Listening to the Journey (30 minutes)

  • In silence, you and the counsellor will listen together to the audio recording of the journey.
  • This active listening helps to expand awareness and anchor the messages received.

4. Integration and Sharing (30 minutes)

  • A closing conversation about the symbols, feelings, and experiences that emerged during the journey.
  • This integration time will also be recorded so that you can revisit it later if you wish.

About the Recordings

  • Two audio recordings will be made: one of the journey and another of the integration.
  • Both will be sent to you, and after being shared, they will be permanently deleted from the counsellor’s devices.

Paths of the Journey

  • If you already have experience with shamanic practices, know your power animals or spirit guides, the journey can begin in different worlds.
  • Otherwise, we will start with the lower world, where you usually meet your first power animal or spiritual allies who will accompany your process.

After the Session (Post-Journey Care)

  • Set aside a quiet time after the session. Avoid intense commitments, meetings, or travel that might disperse your energy.
  • Drink plenty of water and, if possible, have a light meal.
  • Practices such as journalling, walking in nature, or taking a herbal bath can help integrate the experience more deeply.
  • The journey continues to unfold in the following days, so pay attention to dreams, signs, and subtle sensations.

Optional Follow-up

  • You may schedule a short follow-up conversation in the following days (15 to 20 minutes) if you wish to clarify questions or explore insights that arose after the session.

Preparation for the Shamanic Counseling Session

This guide aims to support you in preparing for your Shamanic Counseling session, helping you to reach a state of deep relaxation, clarity, and connection throughout the experience.

1. Detox and Physical Preparation (2 to 3 days before the session)

  • Begin a gentle detoxification process for both body and mind.
  • Avoid stimulants such as coffee, energy drinks, alcohol, and other substances that overstimulate the nervous system.
  • Reduce or eliminate processed or heavy-to-digest foods such as fried or sugary items.
  • Choose light, natural meals that support balance and comfort during the journey.

2. Clarity of Intention

  • Take some time to reflect on what you wish to understand, transform, or deepen during the session.
  • Having a clear intention helps to guide the journey with more focus and depth.
  • The first 30 minutes of the session will be dedicated to sharing and, if needed, refining your intention with the counsellor.

3. Timing and Cancellations

  • Morning sessions are recommended, as the mind is naturally clearer and the body more receptive, with fewer accumulated stimuli.
  • In case you need to cancel or reschedule, please notify at least 24 to 48 hours in advance.
  • Cancellations made with less than 24 hours’ notice may be subject to a partial charge or no refund, depending on the case.

4. Personal Items and Comfort

  • If you wish, you may bring your own headphones and an eye mask to soften the light and support deeper focus. Don’t worry — headphones and eye masks are available at the session if needed.
  • Wear comfortable clothing that does not restrict movement or cause discomfort.

5. Arrival and Preparation

  • Arrive at least 15 minutes early to use the restroom, drink water, and settle into the energy of the space.
  • Put your phone in aeroplane mode or switch it off to avoid interruptions.
  • The session is confidential; only the client and the counsellor should be present.

6. Consent and Safety

By participating in the session, you acknowledge that this is a process of self-awareness and expanded consciousness, not a clinical or diagnostic treatment. If you are currently experiencing acute emotional distress, using psychoactive substances, or under psychiatric care, please inform in advance so the process can be adapted safely.
