About

From Crisis to Calling

I didn't choose this path. It chose me.

I lost two jobs in a row. The financial pressure was immense — bills to pay, a family depending on me, no clear way forward. It was the darkest period of my life.

It was only when I stopped fighting and accepted the calling with my whole heart that everything began to change. Today I bring together transpersonal psychology, shamanic traditions and sound — creating a grounded, compassionate space where honest change, healing and authentic growth can take place.

I know what it feels like to be lost. And I know what it feels like when clarity finally arrives.
