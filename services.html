For those ready to receive guidance directly from their own inner wisdom and helping spirits.

  • Intention — 30 minutes to define and clarify your question or intention.
  • Journey — 30 minutes of narrated shamanic journey with drum.
  • Review — 30 minutes listening back to the recorded journey.
  • Integration — 30 minutes to reflect, process and ground what emerged.

Prerequisite: prior experience with shamanic journeying. Please complete the pre‑requisite form before booking.

Programme

Journey of Reconnection & Expansion of Consciousness

4 – 6 weeks €600

For those ready to go beyond a single session — and enter a full cycle of transformation, integration and renewal.

This is not a workshop or a course. It is a living process — three therapeutic encounters woven together with continuous support, sacred ceremony and personalised integration. Each stage prepares the ground for the next, allowing something real and lasting to emerge.

The Three Encounters

  • Session I — Cacao & Shamanic Counseling
    We open the field with sacred cacao — a heart-centred plant medicine that softens the inner defences and prepares the ground. From there, we enter the Shamanic Counseling method (based on Michael Harner's approach), where the answers come from your own direct experience and inner wisdom — not from the practitioner. What surfaces here sets the intention for the whole journey.
  • Session II — Sacred Plant Medicine & Sound Journey
    A deepening. We work with a second plant ally to clarify and strengthen what emerged in the first session. This is followed by a live sound bath and 30 minutes of live drumming — creating a sustained altered state that allows the unconscious to speak more clearly. What the mind cannot reach, the body and sound will find.
  • Session III — Ancestral Medicine & Integration Ceremony
    The closing ceremony. Working with a sacred Amazonian plant medicine used for centuries to clear, ground and restore inner clarity, we complete the energetic cycle opened in the first session. This final encounter is dedicated to integration: expanding, anchoring and making meaning of all that was experienced across the journey.

Continuous Support Throughout

The journey does not end when the session closes — it expands into your daily life, your dreams, your relationships. Between sessions and throughout the full programme, you have direct access for questions, reflections and ongoing guidance. All sessions are recorded and shared with you. At the end of the process, you receive a personalised written integration document — a map of your journey, the symbols, animals and patterns that emerged, and practical guidance for sustaining what was opened.

Session IV — Anchoring (Online)

Three to four weeks after the final session, we meet online for a dedicated integration session of 60–90 minutes. This is where we harvest what has grown — reviewing your dreams, your shifts, what has settled and what is still moving. A gentle ceremony closes the cycle and we set a conscious intention for the path forward.

Who This Is For

This programme is for those navigating a significant life transition, carrying patterns that keep repeating, or feeling a deep call toward something they cannot yet name. No prior experience with plant medicine or shamanic work is required — only genuine intention and readiness to meet yourself with honesty.

Investment: €600 — includes all four sessions, continuous support throughout the programme, session recordings and personalised integration document. A free 20-minute discovery call is required before booking.

* All plant medicines used within this programme are of traditional and ceremonial origin. Their use is spiritual and ceremonial in nature — not medical. All work is conducted with full informed consent. Please disclose any allergies, medications or health conditions in the pre-requisite form.
